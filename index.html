LOGOS

In this world, God is not a ruler but a perfectly compressed sentence. Matter is what remains after that sentence unzips into time. The Scriptorium is sealed because grief has become architecture. You are not here to cast spells. You are here to perform syntactic surgery on reality.

Axiom
The Logos is a point of absolute compression. Every object is a noisy decompression of a truer, shorter meaning.
Method
Activate the Lens, read the hidden stories, extract a concept from the mirror, load it into the Astrolabe, and impart it into the correct target.
Controls
WASD walks around the room. Left Drag rotates the view. Left Click selects an object. 1 Observe. 2 Isolate. 3 Imprint. E uses the current instrument. Space toggles Lens Mode. Tab hides the interface.
Objective
Open the shutter, extract MEMORY_OF_LIGHT, awaken the seed into a bloom, then use CONCEPT_OF_MORNING to resolve the roots and open the door.
